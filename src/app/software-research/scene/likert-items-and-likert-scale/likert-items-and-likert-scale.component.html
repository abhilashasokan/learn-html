Types of measurement scales

Likert item, formulated by Rensis Likert in 1932, is one of the most commonly used method of attitude measurement.

An example is given below.

Give your agreement to the following statement:
Students spend more time on social media than with text book :
Strongly Agree / Agree /Neutral /Disagree/Strongly Disagree.
This gives the respondents enough options to choose from. Likert items are considered as Ordinal.

Likert scale is a combination of multiple Likert Items. E.g. Satisfaction derived from a product measured using 3 Likert items based on Quality, Price, and after sales service.

A Likert scale, satisfaction based on the 3 Likert Items, e.g., an average of the values (3 Likert items) is generally be considered as Interval scale.
