History of Internet

In 1957, the Soviet Union launched the first satellite, Sputnik I, triggering 'US Government to create the ARPA (Advanced Research Projects Agency) to regain the technological lead in the arms race and help protect the US against a space-based nuclear attack. This resulted countrywide communications network, ARPANET, scientists at ARPANET realized the potential benefits Of network and the internet was born. In 1990, the ARPANET was retired and transferred to the National Science Foundation Network (NSFNET). The NSFNET was soon connected to the Computer Science Network (CSNET), which linked Universities around North America, and then to the European Network (EUnet) which connected research facilities in Europe. The online world changed into its current recognizable form in 1990, when computer scientist Tim Berners-Lee invented the World Wide Web (WWW). The use of the Internet increased exponentially after 1990 causing the US Government to transfer management to independent organizations starting in 1995 and thereafter the internet grew in proportions to the present state.

Benefits of the Internet

The Internet is one of the greatest achievements of humankind. It has transformed the way we live. Some of the most significant benefits are given below

  1. Information, knowledge, and learning

    Internet contains the source of endless supply of knowledge and information that allows a person to learn about anything. It can give answers to any question a person can have. There are also millions of videos on sites like YouTube that help explain various topics and Massive Online Course (MOOC) which has courses on a variety oc subjects, which are offered by the top universities of E.g. edx.org, coursera.org, udemy.com

  2. Communication, Connectivity and Sharing

    It is possible to e-mail or chat with anyone from any part of the world real time. There are thousands of online forums for people of common interests where each one can chat with people who they have not seen in person, to share their knowledge, experiences and ideas.

  3. Internet of Things

    Internet of Things (IoT) is the concept of connecting any device to the Internet and to other connected devices which collect and share data about the way they are used and about the environment around them. It includes a wide variety of smart devices, from industrial machines that transmit data about the production process to sensors that track information about the human body. The devices become smarter and more efficient and help save money, and time.

  4. Cloud computing and cloud storage

    Cloud computing and cloud storage is the practice of using a network of remote servers which are hosted on the Internet to store, manage, and process data, instead of a local server or a personal computer. With cloud computing, a device will have access to more powerful computers and even super computers to perform complex tasks and Cloud storage allows you to have access to an endless supply of storage and also makes backing up data easier and safer.

  5. Address, mapping, and contact information

    The Internet can find directions to almost every place in the world. Transportation network companies like Uber rely on the internet to successfully complete passenger rides. It is very easy to find out the address of establishments institution anywhere in the world. Nowadays Google will detect your location and customize your results based 00 your location. E.g. A search for restaurants nearby, given results based on the location.

  6. Banking and shopping

    The E-Banking gives you access to your bank account to view, make transactions via Internet and Mobile Banking, Online shopping offers the advantage and convenience to find and purchase products without visiting a store. The Internet gives access to compare prices and refer online reviews to make better purchasing decisions.

  7. Selling and making money

    Internet is the easiest and the perfect place to sell most goods. It is easy for the customer to find the website and place orders. The website will be available 24x7 and with the development in E-Commerce, doing business online opens a world of opportunities.

  8. Entertainment

    The Internet paves the way for unending supply of entertainment, music, videos games etc. People who are talented can record and upload their video. Depending on the number of views and the number of advertisements viewed in between, it can become a source of income.

  9. Donations and crowd funding

    Crowd funding is the method of funding a project or venture by requesting via the Internet to large OC people to contribute small amounts of money, With advent of Internet it is easy to make contributions and monitor the progress of projects and ventures. It is convenient for the people who need funding and for the people who are interested in contributing to such funds.
