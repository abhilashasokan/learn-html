Disadvantages/ Drawbacks of the Internet

Internet is not free from drawbacks and disadvantages. Given below are some of the drawbacks of Internet.

  1. Identity theft, hacking, viruses, and cheating

    The Internet makes it easier for hackers to scan millions of computers and quickly identify which computers are vulnerable to attack. A series of cyber-crimes like unauthorized access & Hacking, Trojan Attack, Virus and Worm attack, Email Spoofing, Intellectual Property Right Violations, Banking/ Credit card Related crimes, Phishing, Cyber Terrorism etc. are the result of internet revolution. The Internet also makes it much easier for students to plagiarise On their academic work.

  2. Spam and advertising

    Since internet is an easy way to reach a larger audience, it can be used for unsolicited advertisements and spams. Junk mail or e-mail spam, are unsolicited emails sent to a large number of persons.

  3. Cyber bullying, trolls, stalkers, and crime

    Cyber bullying refers to the use of internet to bully a person by sending threatening messages. Troll refers to creating an online post which is deliberately offensive or provocative with the aim of eliciting an angry response or causing upset to another person. Stalking refers to following a person online.

  4. Buying products not required

    As E- Commerce makes it easy to buy things, deals are very common. Each deal and offer will only for a short period. This tempt people into impulsive buying behaviour.

  5. Never being able to disconnect

    With all the gadgets and devices that people possess it is difficult to disconnect form the Internet. People are always online from one device or the other.

  6. Addiction, time waster, and causes distractions

    The Internet and online gaming can become very addictive often causing users to waste time without doing anything constructive. Internet offers lots of distractions that take away the productive time.

  7. Depression, loneliness, and social isolation

    People who are active on Social networking start comparing their lives with others. They will be concerned about likes and shares instead of focusing on their own lives. This pushes people into social isolation and loneliness.

  8. Exploitation and pornography and violent images

    Through the internet, it becomes easy to be exploited a known or unknown person. Fraudsters can create accounts and trap innocent gullible people. The Interne( is also full of adult materials that can be easily by people unintentionally.

  9. Health issues and obesity

    Spending too much time on the computer or mobile phone can lead to an unhealthy life. Extensive use of computer or mobile has a lot of repetitive movements that can lead to carpal tunnel syndrome which is having numbness and weakness in your hands.
