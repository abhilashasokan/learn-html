Links

Links are elements in a web page which can be selected by clicking on it. Links generally appear as underlined texts or icons. When we click on to this link we are transferred to another HTML page, sound, video files etc. The resource that we are intending to access may be located on our computer's hard disk or on the intranet site or anywhere on the World Wide Web.

Definition lists are unique among lists owing to the reason that they offer two levels of list items within the listed structure-one for defined term and the other for definition. dl stands for definition list and dt stands for definition term and dd for definition itself.
